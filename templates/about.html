Our Methodology

We have developed a structure for your learning journey, guided by domain experts. You can adapt it for your role or organisation. We have found this method to be engaging, enthusiastic and captivating. Our learning is loosely split between 5 key themes:

  1. How healthcare works
  2. Humans in healthcare
  3. Clinical healthcare
  4. Money in healthcare
  5. Being part of a healthcare system
